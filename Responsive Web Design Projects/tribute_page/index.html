Emperor Haile Selassie

The Emperor of Ethiopia Haile Selassie

Here's a time line of Emperor Haile Selassie

July 23 1892

Tafari Makonnen Woldemikael born in a village of Ejersa Goro in the Harar province of Ethiopia.

November 1 1905

At the age of 13 Tafari was given the title "Commander of the gate" roughly equivalent to "count"

March 21 1906

Selassie's visits his father Ras Makonnen on his death bed at Kulib battling Typhus Fever. As the daylight broke giving his son Tafari a whispered blessing as he passed away.

August 3 1911

Tafari Makonnen Woldemikael married Wayzaro Menen Asfaw, great granddaughter of Menilek II.

September 28 1923

Ethiopia joins the membership of the League of Nations by promising to eradicate slavery.

1928

Crowned King of Shewa kingdom by Empress Zewditu.

November 2 1930

Becomes Emperror Haile Selassie and uses his Ge'ez name when Empress Zewditu succumbs to diabetes.

July 16 1931

Enacting the country's first constituition. This was the first instance in history where an absolute ruler had sought voluntarily to share sovereign power with the subjects of his realm.

October 3 1935

Italizan forces cross the border into Ethiopia from Eritrea using aerial warfare and poison gas, three days after Haile Selassie has left the country on the Imperial Railway.

April 9 1936

Haile Selassie arrives in Geneva where he addressed the League of Nations. "It is us today, It will be you tomorrow."

May 5 1936

Selassie goes into exile.

October 14 1940

Selassie eldest daughter Princess Romanework Haile Selassie dies in captivity at Turin. Possibly of tuberculosis.

January 18 1941

Ethiopian forces exiled in Sudan and the help selassie secured from the British invaded Ethiopia.

May 5 1941

Haile Selassie returns to Ethiopia. Following the italian withdrawl from africa after heavy losses

January 31 1942

Britain hands back full sovereignty to Ethiopia with Anglo-Ethiopian agreement signed.

December 13 1960

The council of the Revolution led by 4 brothers. The coup leaders declared the beginning of a new government under the rule of Haile Selassie's eldest son, Crown Prince Asfaw Wossen

October 4 1963

Selassie held principles of international cooperation and collective responsibility underline his found of the Organisation of African Unity.

April 21 1966

Haile Selassie plane is swamped by an enthusiastic crowd as it lands in Jamaica on April 21st, 1966. By those of the Rastafarian religion that bears the Emperor's birth name who believe his visit to be the coming ofthe black messiah. April 21st is still celebrated by Rastafarians as Grounation Day.

1973

According to a 1974 UN FAO report 300,000 people predominantly the northern rural poor died during the 1973-1974 Ethiopian famine.

September 12 1974

Selassie is deposed bringin the long-reigning Solomonic dynasty to an end.

August 27 1975

Selassie died the cause of death is officially given as respiratory failure, but many believe he was killed by the members of the new military government.
